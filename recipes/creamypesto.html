Creamy Pesto

Ingredients

  • Pesto
  • Garlic
  • Pasta
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Cook 3 minced cloves of garlic in a pan with olive oil on medium heat til brown.
  2. Boil pasta for 6-10 minutes.
  3. Lower heat and mix in pesto, and heavy cream in the pan with the garlic.
  4. Simmer til creamy
  5. Mix the cooked pasta in with the sauce in the pan.
  6. Enjoy!
