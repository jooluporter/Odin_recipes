Alfredo Pasta

Quick and easy Alfredo sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 6-10 minutes
  2. Save a cup of pasta water
  3. Once pasta is strained mix it in a pan on low heat with a stick of butter, a cup of grated parmesan, and pasta water until creamy
